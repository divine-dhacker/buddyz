FAQ
What is Buddyz?

Buddyz is a free friendship quiz platform. You answer 15 fun questions about yourself, get a unique link, and send it to friends. Each friend guesses your answers, and a live leaderboard shows who knows you best.

How do I create a Buddyz quiz?

Tap "Create My Quiz", enter your name, and answer 15 quick questions about your habits, tastes and memories. Buddyz instantly generates a unique link you can share with friends — no sign-up or download required.

Is Buddyz free to use?

Yes — Buddyz is completely free, with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Anyone with the link can take a quiz at no cost.

Do my friends need to download anything?

No. Buddyz runs entirely in the browser. Friends just open your link, type their name, answer the questions, and instantly see their score and tier on the leaderboard.

How long does my quiz link stay active?

Each quiz link stays active for 7 days from creation. After that, it expires automatically and friends will see an expiry screen instead of the quiz.

Is my data private on Buddyz?

Yes. Buddyz doesn't require accounts, passwords, or personal data beyond the name you choose to enter. Quiz answers and leaderboard scores are only accessible to people who have your unique quiz link.

Who created Buddyz?

Buddyz was created by Divine Nosakhare, the Founder & CEO of Buddyz — a self-taught developer from Nigeria who builds the entire platform using JavaScript and Firebase.
