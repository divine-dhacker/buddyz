What is Buddyz and how does it work?

The Buddyz is a fun and interactive way to test just how well your friends know you. You can create personalized questions about your habits, preferences, and memories, and share the quiz link with your circle. Friends attempt to guess correctly, and their scores show who’s truly paying attention!

Whether it’s nostalgic questions about your favorite childhood show or quirky facts like your secret midnight snack, the questions are tailored by you. Once people take the quiz, their scores are ranked so you can see who knows you best. It’s a fun way to strengthen friendships and stir up some good-natured competition.

No apps, no downloads needed. Just create, share, and watch the results roll in. Simple, safe, and guaranteed to spark some laughs.
