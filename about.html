My Story

My name is Divine, a 17-year-old self-taught developer from Nigeria with a strong passion for technology, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving. I started my journey with HTML and CSS before moving into JavaScript and full-stack development. Today, I'm the Founder & CEO of Buddyz, a free friendship quiz platform I designed, built, and ship entirely on my own.

My goal is not just to learn how to code — but to become a technology founder capable of creating products that solve meaningful problems in Africa and around the world. One of my biggest ambitions is to build a large-scale social platform called Update, designed to connect people in entirely new ways.

My learning philosophy is simple: build more, consume less. Rather than spending all my time on tutorials, I create real projects that teach me through experience. Consistency, curiosity, and persistence matter more than expensive resources.

🤝 What is Buddyz?

Buddyz is one of those real-world projects. I built it to make friendship more fun and shareable — create a quiz about yourself, send one link, and find out who truly knows you best.

No accounts. No downloads. No fees. Just 15 questions, one unique link, and a leaderboard that reveals everything. Built entirely by a 17-year-old with JavaScript and Firebase.
